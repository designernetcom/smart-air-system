VRF V S - SIDE DISCHARGE VRF SYSTEM

CONSERVES ENERGY. SAVES SPACE. ENHANCES AESTHETICS.

The Blue Star VRF V S is an inverter compressor-driven VRF system, designed with compact side discharge outdoor units. This system is very suitable for applications with limited floor space for installing Outdoor Units (ODUs) or for residential applications where aesthetics and space both matter or where there is a slab above the ODUs. Varieties of indoor units complement compact ODUs to offer excellent solutions for air conditioning residences, offices, banks, hotels, restaurants, hospitals, healthcare centres, commercial spaces, etc. VRF V S is a heat pump system, which can be used for both cooling and heating requirements.
