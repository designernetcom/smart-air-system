WATER COOLED CENTRIFUGAL CHILLERS

Blue Star, India's largest central air conditioning company, has been providing expert cooling solutions for over six decades. It has been manufacturing a wide range of scroll, screw and centrifugal chillers. Drawing from this expertise, Blue Star offers a wide range of both air cooled and water cooled scroll chillers with R22, R407c and R410a refrigerant options.

These chillers are manufactured at Blue Star's own ISO 9001 certified factory, with the chiller test bench certified by the Air Conditioning, Heating and Refrigeration Institute (AHRI). Blue Star scroll chillers, which are equipped with multiple hermetic scroll compressors, are ideal for various load applications like medium office spaces, hotels, hospitals, industries, etc.

    SALIENT FEATURES
  • Wide range: 300 - 4000TR
  • Semi-hermetic centrifugal compressor
  • Single and double stage options
  • Multiple scroll compressors
  • Environment-friendly R134a refrigerant
  • VFD option for better part load efficiency
